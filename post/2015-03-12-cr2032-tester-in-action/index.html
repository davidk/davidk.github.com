The CR2032 tester

12 March 2015

The coin cell doesn’t have to be inserted completely for the tester to work, which reduces test time (no need to mess up/bend fingernails prying the battery out too). The next best thing to add to this design would probably be a plastic non-conductive ejection system. And a way to properly attach a load (most batteries are tested under load).
