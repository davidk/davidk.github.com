A modular Pi camera enclosure

01 May 2017

TL;DR: I made a modular Raspberry Pi camera case.

It is now released!

This was mostly borne out of wanting to position the camera head while keeping the cabling as tidy as possible.

And while printed joints are pretty cool to design, quality metal fasteners are still the way to go. Small printed bits just can’t be torqued to a reasonable tightness without shearing or breaking in some way.

So for this particular case M2.5 fasteners are used. And a US-based source for the M2.5 hardware needed is provided in the documentation.

N.B. With increasing joint numbers, the camera will oscillate slightly if moved (there isn’t very much weight/bracing/dampening to the design), so be aware of that.
