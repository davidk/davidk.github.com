About

31 May 2014

Hello, i’m Dave (and/or: davidk, david).

I build cloud tooling and cloud infrastructure (bare metal on up) as a day job, mostly tailored to all things Linux.

This bit holds nearly all of my spare time projects; mostly consisting of printables, software, and hardware.

Popular Posts

This is some of the stuff that has been oddly popular over time. I can’t promise the content is up to date as things move faster than I often expect.

  • Raspberry Flavored Time (A NTP server on your Pi, tethered to a GPS unit)

  • Electroscope - a toy electroscope based off of vk2zay’s design - lets you visualize static charge (along with its polarity) more easily. Great at winter parties.

3D Printables

I build a lot of enclosures for breakout boards, as I live in a particularly dusty area. Maybe you’ve seen or used one in the wild? (If you have, let me know!)

Check out Thingiverse and YouMagine.

The Github organization, Collector3 also has version controlled copies.

Most things will appear on Collector3 first since they don’t need full documentation + photos and act as a place where designs can be viewed/verified as they evolve over time.
