About

31 May 2014

Hello, i’m Dave (I also go by david/davidk).

I build cloud tooling and cloud infrastructure (bare metal on up) as a day job, mostly tailored to all things Linux.

This bit holds nearly all of my spare time projects; mostly consisting of printables, software, and hardware.

3D Printables

I build a lot of enclosures for breakout boards, as my workspace gets dusty. Maybe you’ve seen or used one in the wild? (If you have, let me know!)

Check out Thingiverse and YouMagine.

The Github organization, Collector3 also has version controlled copies.

Most things will appear on Collector3 first since they don’t need full documentation + photos and act as a place where designs can be viewed/verified as they evolve over time.
